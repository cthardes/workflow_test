Summary of bag 2 paths and continuations

PATH STRUCTURE

The advantages of Antifate is that it has many congruents to avoid excessive soft-dropping and covers effectively all bags when paired with Beacon.

Cover: 89.72%

Not recommended to mirror

Antifate is a very old opener, first documented in 2013 by TSTnb on the Harddrop forums. The continuations I made transition into 6-3 stacking.

One can consider using Incomplete Gassho as an alternative as it is much simpler and has chances to converge to the same boardstate.

ANTIFATE TSD

With early T, both TSS and Tetris can be performed in the same bag, continuing into 6-3 or downstacking.

Afterwards, a forecast is always possible. Once Tetris is cleared taking TSS or TSD are both viable options. A minimal amount of setups are shown above if needed.

This path only requires one soft-drop at most and subsequent setups have almost none. Consider using it when J+L are early, a queue disadvantageous to the Shachiku path.
